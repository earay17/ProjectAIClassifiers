Attention Warmblood Enthusiasts!

We’d like to introduce you to WarmBlood Magazine!

WarmBlood Magazine delivers six exciting full color issues per year on warmblood breed registries and inspections, including the know how from top warmblood breeders around the world and in our own back yard.

WarmBlood Magazine covers top sporthorses as they compete in the world of Dressage, Show Jumping, Three-Day, Driving and Hunters. Learn about the results, the lineage and who got them there!

WarmBlood Magazine studies bloodlines, discusses the mare base, tracks stallion approvals and presents the results of our breeding efforts. All this in a full color glossy magazine packed full of information for every warmblood enthusiasts.

As you well know, warmblood sporthorses are uniquely different from other horses. They have exceptional athleticism but their slow maturity requires different training techniques. Information on bloodlines can be hard to come by and the approval process can be confusing, especially for the novice.

With WarmBlood Magazine, you can learn about important foundation stallions and mares in our BLOODLINES column. Or, learn more about aspects of a registry in our BREED SPECIFIC section. Study sporthorse candidates along with Hilda Gurney in WHAT’S MY CONFORMATION?. And, if you are brave enough, debate hotly contested topics in the POINT NOTED column. 

Subscribe today to ensure that you get every exciting issue delivered directly to your door!
