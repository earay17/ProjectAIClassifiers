Softwall Equipment Corporation Pursues Australian Joint Venture
SANDY, Utah, Nov. 28 /PRNewswire/ -- Softwall Equipment Corporation (OTC Bulletin Board: SWLL - news) of Sandy, Utah has accepted an invitation to enter negotiations for joint venture agreement with Tunnel Construction Pty Limited of N.S.W. Australia. The purpose of the joint venture would be to introduce SACUM Conveyor Technology to the Australian mining industry. Mr. Randall Peterson, president of Softwall Equipment Corporation, is currently in Australia negotiating the terms of the joint venture.

SACUM Conveyor technology, owned by Softwall equipment Corporation, is protected by US patent number 6,086,159 with patents pending in Australia and other countries of the world. The SACUM Conveyor introduces several advantages to high wall mining, one of the most common operations used for mining in Australia. Those advantages include but are not limited to increased resource recovery, lower capital investment, reduced operating costs, and increased safety over conventional high wall mining.
