Universe2U and Aetix Group Agree On Terms of Acquisition
Our Business Strategy of Turn-Key Solutions Will Continue
RICHMOND HILL, Ontario--(BUSINESS WIRE)--December 20, 2000-- Universe2U (OTC BB:UTOU - news) Universe2U Inc. today announces the company has entered into an agreement in principle with the Aetix Group (www.aetix.com) of Companies to acquire its Canadian Companies: Business Link Technologies Inc.; Fundamental Computing Inc.; 9075425 Ontario Limited; and it US Companies: Jayce Technologies Inc.; and Intellitech Consulting Inc. 

Strategically located in four major technology sites in Canada and the United States, Aetix facilitates the development of information technology architectures and strategies, and designs and implements global and enterprise networking solutions for diverse local and multi-national clients. In addition to superior technical services, Aetix provides structured project administration, contract services for LAN/MAN/WAN management, eCommerce solutions, ASP hosting and support services. 

In speaking about the merits of the acquisition Kim Allen, CEO Universe2U, ``Universe2U differentiates itself by fully understanding that to build a valuable network asset requires more than a good idea. Once the decision to implement a network is made the decisions don't stop there. The Aetix Group is well positioned to provide superior value-added services that turn the network into a product. Aetix is grounded in a tradition of leadership in the design, integration and support of high-end enterprise class network and information technology solutions. Aetix is a profitable and expanding organization that specializes in first identifying then leveraging selected technologies to eliminate process barriers. We expect Aetix to be a tremendous addition to our team and expand upon our turnkey solution strategy.'' 

The Aetix group has strategically located offices in Canada and the United States. The head office in Toronto contains both a sales and consultant group. The Kitchener, Ontario office space houses the development, hosting services and network management groups. Aetix's presence in Philadelphia providing a base for implementation teams along the eastern seaboard of the U.S. Its Lincoln Nebraska presence offers a strategic base of development and implementation services in the U.S. mid-west. 

Universe2U is focused on helping customers meet their strategic business goals through the proper use of enabling technologies. We facilitate the development of information technology architectures and strategies, provide structured project management, design/implement global and enterprise networking solutions, supplement clients resources by providing negotiating skills on contracts and terms with vendors/carriers, design/build eCommerce solutions and provide contract services for LAN/MAN/WAN management, disaster recovery and helpdesk services. 

With experience in multi-carrier, multi-vendor voice and data network architecture, design and build projects for multi-national organizations, Universe2U combined with the Aetix Group expects to be in a strong position to help new and existing customers to not build just their network, but build them for success. 

The Aetix Group will allow Universe2U to service national and inter-national clients with a seamless approach and methodology. Aetix has previously provided turnkey solutions to clients in Ireland, the U.K., Germany, Bermuda and Singapore.

About Universe2U Inc. 

Universe2U (www.universe2u.com) provides electronic connectivity solutions to communities, communications carriers, building owners, and corporate and government customers in North America. The company is a facilities-based provider of advanced fiber optic and ``managed broadband'' solutions. Universe2U is comprised of seven operating subsidiaries focused on engineering and design, infrastructure installation and maintenance, marketing services, and network services. 

Universe2U expects to pursue a two-prong business strategy: to design and build fiber optic networks and market telecommunication services for major telephone and cable television companies; and to be a pioneer in developing infostructure ``SmartCommunities'' networks in partnership with local governments, ``SmartBuilding'' networks in partnership with institutions and businesses, and ``SmartLinks'' in partnership with right-of-way owners.
